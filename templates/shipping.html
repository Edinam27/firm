Shipping & Delivery Policy

Delivery Areas

Pure Water currently delivers to the following areas:

  • Metropolitan areas within a 50-mile radius of our distribution centers
  • Select suburban and rural areas (check availability with your zip code)

To check if we deliver to your area, please enter your zip code on our products page or contact our customer service team.

Delivery Timeframes

For one-time orders:

  • Standard Delivery: 2-3 business days
  • Express Delivery: Next business day (order by 2 PM)
  • Same-Day Delivery: Available in select areas (order by 10 AM)

For subscription deliveries:

  • Deliveries are made according to your selected frequency (weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly)
  • You can select your preferred delivery day during subscription setup
  • We'll notify you via email or text message the day before your scheduled delivery

Shipping Fees

Our shipping fees are calculated based on:

  • Delivery location
  • Order volume
  • Delivery speed

Standard shipping rates:

  • Orders under $30: $5.99 shipping fee
  • Orders $30-$50: $3.99 shipping fee
  • Orders over $50: FREE standard shipping

Additional fees may apply for:

  • Express delivery: +$8.99
  • Same-day delivery: +$12.99
  • Remote locations: Additional fees may apply

Subscription customers: All subscription plans include FREE standard delivery.

Delivery Process

What to expect on delivery day:

  • Our delivery personnel will bring your order to your door
  • For water cooler bottles and heavy items, we offer placement service to your preferred location
  • Empty bottles can be left outside for collection during your next delivery

Special instructions:

  • You can add delivery instructions during checkout
  • If you need to change delivery instructions, please contact us at least 24 hours before delivery
  • For apartment buildings or offices, please ensure we have proper access information

Tracking Your Order

Stay updated on your delivery:

  • You'll receive a confirmation email when your order is placed
  • A tracking notification will be sent when your order is out for delivery
  • For subscription customers, you'll receive a reminder email the day before your scheduled delivery

You can also track your order status by:

  • Logging into your account on our website
  • Using our mobile app
  • Contacting our customer service team

Delivery Issues

If you experience any issues with your delivery:

  • Contact our customer service team within 24 hours
  • For damaged products, please take photos if possible
  • If you miss a delivery, we'll leave a notification and attempt redelivery on the next business day

For any questions about our shipping and delivery policy, please contact us.
