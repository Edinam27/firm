Returns Policy

Our Commitment to Quality

At Pure Water, we stand behind the quality of our products. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help.

Return Policy for Bottled Water Products

Due to the nature of our products and for health and safety reasons, we generally do not accept returns on opened water products. However, we offer the following options:

Damaged or Defective Products

If you receive a damaged or defective product:

  • Contact our customer service team within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide your order number and a description of the issue
  • We may request photos of the damaged products
  • We'll arrange for a replacement or refund

Unopened Products

For unopened products in their original packaging:

  • Returns must be initiated within 14 days of delivery
  • Products must be in their original, sealed condition
  • Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to our error

Return Policy for Equipment and Accessories

For water dispensers, coolers, and other equipment:

  • 30-day return period from the date of delivery
  • Items must be in original packaging with all accessories
  • A 15% restocking fee may apply
  • Customer is responsible for return shipping costs

Subscription Cancellations

For subscription services:

  • You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account dashboard
  • Cancellations must be made at least 3 business days before your next scheduled delivery
  • No refunds are provided for partial subscription periods

How to Initiate a Return

To initiate a return or report a problem with your order:

  1. Contact our customer service team at support@purewater.com or call (800) 123-4567
  2. Provide your order number and details about your return request
  3. Our team will guide you through the next steps

Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will process your refund:

  • Refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days
  • Credit will be applied to your original payment method
  • You will receive an email notification when your refund is processed

If you have any questions about our return policy, please contact us.
