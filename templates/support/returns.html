Returns Policy

Our Satisfaction Guarantee

At PureFlow, we stand behind the quality of our products. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help.

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee on all our products. If you're not happy with your purchase for any reason, you can return it within 30 days for a full refund or replacement.

Eligible Returns

The following items are eligible for returns:

  • Unopened water bottles and containers
  • Dispensers and accessories in original packaging
  • Damaged or defective products

Please note:

  • Opened consumable products cannot be returned for safety and quality reasons
  • Custom or personalized items may not be eligible for return
  • Sale items may have limited return eligibility

Return Process

  1. Contact Us: Initiate your return by contacting our customer service team through the Contact page or by calling (800) 555-WATER.
  2. Return Authorization: You'll receive a Return Authorization (RA) number and return instructions.
  3. Package Your Return: Securely package the items in their original packaging if possible.
  4. Ship or Schedule Pickup: Either ship the items back using the provided return label or schedule a pickup with our delivery team.
  5. Refund Processing: Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund or replacement.

Refunds

Refunds will be processed to the original payment method used for the purchase:

  • Credit/debit card refunds typically take 5-7 business days to appear on your statement
  • Other payment methods may take longer to process

Shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error or a defective product.

Damaged or Defective Products

If you receive a damaged or defective product:

  1. Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  2. Provide photos of the damaged items if possible
  3. We'll arrange for a replacement or refund at no additional cost

For subscription customers, we'll ensure your next delivery includes any replacement items needed.

Subscription Cancellations

You can cancel your subscription at any time through your account dashboard:

  • No cancellation fees or penalties
  • Prorated refunds for unused portion of prepaid subscriptions
  • Option to pause subscriptions instead of cancelling

For assistance with subscription changes, please contact our customer service team.
