Frequently Asked Questions

You can place an order by browsing our products page, selecting the items you want, and adding them to your cart. Once you're ready to checkout, follow the prompts to complete your purchase. You'll need to create an account or sign in to complete your order.

One-time orders are single purchases delivered once. Subscriptions are recurring deliveries at intervals you choose (weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly). Subscriptions offer convenience and discounted pricing compared to one-time orders.

Standard delivery typically takes 2-3 business days. For subscription customers, we deliver on your scheduled delivery day. You can check your delivery status in your account dashboard after placing an order.

We currently deliver to most major metropolitan areas and surrounding suburbs. You can check if we deliver to your area by entering your zip code during checkout or on our subscription page.

Our water is sourced from carefully selected natural springs known for their purity and mineral content. Each source undergoes rigorous testing to ensure the highest quality standards are met before bottling.

Yes, all our bottles are 100% recyclable. We also offer a bottle return program for subscription customers to reduce environmental impact. Learn more about our sustainability initiatives on our About page.

You can manage your subscription through your account dashboard. Log in to your account, go to "My Subscriptions," and you can modify delivery frequency, skip deliveries, change products, update payment methods, or pause/cancel your subscription.

You can reach our customer support team through our Contact page, by email at support@pureflow.com, or by phone at (800) 555-WATER during business hours (Monday-Friday, 9am-5pm).
