Shipping Information

Delivery Areas

PureFlow currently delivers to the following areas:

  • Major metropolitan areas and surrounding suburbs
  • Select rural areas within our service regions

To check if we deliver to your location, enter your zip code during checkout or on our subscription page.

Delivery Timeframes

One-time Orders:

  • Standard Delivery: 2-3 business days
  • Express Delivery: Next business day (additional fee applies)

Subscription Orders:

  • Delivered on your scheduled delivery day
  • Choose from weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly delivery schedules
  • Ability to customize delivery days based on your preference

Delivery Process

  1. Order Confirmation: You'll receive an email confirmation once your order is placed.
  2. Processing: Orders are typically processed within 24 hours.
  3. Shipping Notification: You'll receive an email with tracking information when your order ships.
  4. Delivery: Our delivery team will bring your order to your doorstep.
  5. Confirmation: For contactless delivery, we'll send a delivery confirmation email.

Shipping Fees

One-time Orders:

  • Orders under $50: $9.95 shipping fee
  • Orders $50-$99.99: $5.95 shipping fee
  • Orders $100+: Free shipping
  • Express Delivery: Additional $15 fee

Subscription Orders:

  • All subscription orders include free standard shipping
  • Express delivery options available for an additional fee

Special Delivery Instructions

You can add special delivery instructions during checkout, such as:

  • Gate codes or building access information
  • Preferred placement location (front door, side entrance, etc.)
  • Delivery time preferences (morning, afternoon, evening)

For subscription customers, you can update your delivery instructions at any time through your account dashboard.
